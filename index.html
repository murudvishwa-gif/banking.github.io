Have Questions?

Answers to what people ask most

Can't find what you need? Our support team replies in under 90 seconds.

Is my money safe with Stackly?
+
Yes. All deposits are insured up to ₹5 lakh per customer under DICGC (Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation). Stackly is regulated by the Reserve Bank of India and holds an AA+ credit rating from CRISIL.
How quickly can I open an account?
+
Most accounts are opened in under 4 minutes using Aadhaar-based eKYC and a selfie. You'll need your Aadhaar, PAN, and a mobile number linked to Aadhaar. Your account number and debit card are issued instantly.
What is the auto-sweep feature?
+
Auto-sweep automatically moves any idle savings balance above a threshold you set (e.g., ₹25,000) into an overnight liquid mutual fund — earning approximately 6.8% annualised. The money sweeps back into your savings account by 9 AM the next day, available for spending at any time.
Can I use Stackly if I live abroad?
+
Yes. NRIs can open NRE, NRO, or FCNR accounts with Stackly. Our dedicated NRI team handles the paperwork, FEMA compliance, and provides preferential remittance rates. You can manage everything through the app from anywhere in the world.
How do I apply for a home loan?
+
Start your home loan application entirely online via the app or website. Our system provides a conditional sanction letter within 15 minutes based on your credit profile. A relationship manager then contacts you within 2 hours to guide the full documentation process. Disbursal typically takes 7-10 working days.
What charges apply for international transactions?
+
Essential plan: 3.5% markup on forex transactions. Premium plan: 1.5% markup. Private plan: interbank rates with zero markup. All international ATM withdrawals on Premium and above are free worldwide.

Our Story

Building India's most trusted digital bank

From a single Andheri office in 2008 to a nationwide force with 2.8M customers — here's how we got here.

Our Mission

To make world-class banking accessible to every Indian — from a vegetable vendor in Varanasi to a startup founder in Bengaluru. We believe financial dignity is a right, not a privilege.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted financial institution in Asia by 2030 — not measured by assets, but by the number of lives improved through better financial health and inclusion.
